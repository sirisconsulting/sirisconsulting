Who we are

We seek to provide our clients with a recruitment service covering all of their needs. If a particular discipline falls outside our core competence then we can satisfy your needs through a comprehensive netwrok of extended suppliers who align with our principles on speed and quality.

Why Choose Us?

At Siris Consulting we pride ourselves on the quality of both our service and the people we put forward. This is why we endeavour to meet our clients and candidates face to face to get a deeper understanding of your business and individual employment needs.

What We Achieve?

Our service model is based around dedicated recruitment practices combined with required training and vetting done by experts in the field. This expert approach ensures an in depth understanding of our clients’ and candidates requirements together with swift and accurate matching of best-fit candidates.

Our Strengths

We partnered with educational institutions and companies in home country of candidates to prepare them for the UK/European standard, to further ensure the job readiness of the candidates before accepting their new job.

Our Aim

Our aim is to work in partnership with all of our applicants, whether they are looking for permanent or temporary positions. We listen, learn and genuinely care about our applicants aspirations by offering them with individual counsuling on career, CV, and interview advice.
